OUR MISSION

To promote fitness, social interaction, teamwork, and sportsmanship in the local community via involvement in community Basketball.

OUR HISTORY

Bayswater Boomers were founded in 1967. We have been an active and popular part of the Bassendean community ever since.

We have been affiliated with Basketball WA since 1982. They manage the schedule for Winter and Summer seasons and also provide us with insurance and policies and procedures for the competition.
